# Design-Partner Outreach

Practical, concise templates for starting design-partner conversations. Keep the
language review-support only: never promise approval, certification, validation,
verification, guaranteed compliance, safety, passing review, or first-pass
acceptance. A human reviewer remains responsible for every item.

## Concise product description

Civil Engineer AI is a pre-submittal QA tool for civil and AEC teams. It helps a
firm review a stormwater or site package before it goes to a municipal reviewer:
it organizes DXF metadata into review-support findings, flags plan and report
conflicts, keeps every issue tied to source evidence, and drafts a reviewer
handoff package. It is a pilot-ready prototype, not production SaaS, and it does
not approve plans or certify compliance.

## Pilot call agenda (20 minutes)

1. Your current pre-submittal QA process and where resubmittals come from (5 min).
2. Live walkthrough of the guided demo on the Brookside Meadows sample (7 min).
3. What is real today: DXF parsing, PDF text-layer indexing, evidence
   traceability; and what is not: no OCR, no DWG/GIS, no vector search (3 min).
4. Whether a scoped pilot on one of your real packages makes sense, and what you
   would want to get out of it (5 min).
